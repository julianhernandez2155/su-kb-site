## **What is Claude Enterprise?**

Claude Enterprise is Syracuse University's premium AI assistant that helps you work smarter, not harder. Think of Claude as your intelligent digital colleague who can help you write, analyze, research, and solve problems across virtually any subject area. [Sign-Up Now!](https://getclaude.syr.edu/)

### **Getting Started: The Basics**

The easiest way to get started is to ask Claude a question or give Claude a task. Here are a few simple ideas:

- **Writing Assistant**: Help draft emails, reports, essays, and creative content
- **Research Partner**: Summarize articles, explain complex topics, and find connections between ideas
- **Problem Solver**: Break down complicated problems, suggest solutions, and help with analysis
- **Learning Companion**: Explain concepts in simple terms, create study guides, and answer questions

### **Best Practices for New Users**

**Be Specific**: The more detail you provide, the better Claude can help. Instead of "help with my essay," try "help me create an outline for a 5-page essay about the impact of social media on democracy."

**Verify Important Information**: While Claude is highly capable, always double-check critical facts, citations, and data for important work.
