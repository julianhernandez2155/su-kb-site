Discover powerful ways to use AI tools beyond basic questions and answers. Each tool has unique capabilities - from building interactive dashboards to generating podcast-style summaries. Below are the superpowers of each tool and creative workflows that combine them in surprising ways.

For full tool details and data policies: [Approved Tools for Use with University Data](./approved-tools-for-use-with-university-data.md)

---

# Tool Superpowers:

### 🧠 Claude - The Builder

> [!info]
> Superpower: Builds interactive tools you can use and share - not just text responses
>
> Unique Capability: Creates live artifacts (calculators, dashboards, visualizations, quizzes) that you edit in real-time
>
> Best For:
>
> - Building interactive tools without coding
> - Deep analysis of long documents (200+ pages)
> - Technical work and code generation
>
> Try It: Ask Claude to "build an interactive grade calculator" or "create a filterable task tracker"
>
> Claude FAQ: [Claude - Frequently Asked Questions](./claude-frequently-asked-questions.md)

---

### 🎙️ Gemini NotebookLM - The Podcast Machine

> [!info]
> Superpower: Turns documents into 10-20 minute AI-generated podcast conversations
>
> Unique Capability: Audio overviews - two AI hosts discuss your content in engaging dialogue format
>
> Best for:
>
> - Making dense content listenable (commute/gym study)
> - Multi-document research with citations
> - Creating audio study guides
>
> Try It: Upload lecture transcripts and generate an audio "course overview"

---

### ⚙️ Microsoft Copilot for Work - The Auto-Documenter

> [!info]
> Superpower: Automatically documents Teams meetings while you focus on participating
>
> Unique Capability: Zero-effort meeting summaries - records, transcribes, and summarizes without manual work
>
> Best for:
>
> - Microsoft 365 integration (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams)
> - Enterprise-level automation
> - Organization-wide search
>
> Where You'll Find Copilot Buttons:
>
> - Microsoft Teams - Meeting transcription, chat summaries, catch-up on missed meetings
> - Word - Draft documents, rewrite sections, summarize long files
> - Excel - Analyze data, create formulas, generate insights
> - PowerPoint - Create presentations from prompts, design slides
> - Outlook - Draft emails, summarize long threads
> - OneNote - Organize notes, create summaries
>
> **⚠️ Important:** Microsoft Copilot for Work requires an M365 Copilot for Work license, which is an additional license that must be purchased.
>
> Try It: Let Copilot for Work watch your meeting, then ask "What were the key decisions?"

# Creative AI Ideas

---
> [!note]- The Audio Time Machine
> Upload entire semester of lecture transcripts to NotebookLM
>
> Generate single course overview podcast (10-20 min)
>
> Listen before finals = comprehensive review on commute
>
> Turn 40 hours of lectures into digestible audio

> [!note]- The Living Dashboard Builder
> Upload meeting notes to Claude
>
> Ask: Build interactive task tracker with filters and progress bars
>
> Get working artifact you can share with team
>
> Update it by talking to Claude, not coding
>
> Project management without the software

> [!note]- The Lecture-to-Flashcard Pipeline
> Record class with permission
>
> Upload recording(s) to mentorAI mentor: Create 30 flashcards + 10 practice questions with answers
>
> Upload same transcript to NotebookLM for audio study guide
>
> Study with both active recall AND passive listening

> [!note]- The Email Project
> Ask Copilot for Work: Find all emails about [project] from last 6 months
>
> Ask Claude: Find all emails about [project] from last 6 months (Requires the enabling of the Microsoft 365 Connector)
>
> Create timeline of decisions made
>
> Draft status update based on this history

> [!note]- The AI Debate Arena
> Claude argues Position A, mentorAI-ChatGPT argues Position B
>
> You moderate by copying responses between them
>
> Watch AIs strengthen each other's arguments
>
> See both sides at their best, not their worst

> [!note]- The Grade Calculator You'll Actually Use
> Upload syllabus to Claude
>
> Ask: Build interactive grade calculator with 'what-if' sliders
>
> Get working tool: If I get 85% on final, what's my grade?
>
> Share link with classmates
>
> Bookmark and use all semester

> [!note]- The Prompt Breeding Lab
> Ask mentorAI: Critique this prompt and improve it
>
> Feed improved version to Claude: Make this even better
>
> Feed that to Gemini for third perspective
>
> Watch prompts evolve across AI ecosystems
>
> Learn what makes prompts actually work

> [!note]- The Research Poster Generator
> Upload paper to Claude: Create conference poster layout in HTML
>
> Get interactive artifact with your data visualized
>
> Edit colors and text in real-time
>
> Export and print = professional poster in 10 minutes

> [!note]- The Zero-Effort Meeting Documentation
> Copilot for Work auto-transcribes your Teams meeting
>
> Upload transcript to Claude: Use Meeting Transcript project
>
> Get shareable task tracker artifact
>
> From meeting to project board in 2 minutes

> [!note]- The Personal Research Assistant
> Create a custom AI assistant with your citation style
>
> Upload papers and have it summarize in your format
>
> Build a library of summaries in consistent style

> [!note]- The Citation Style Switcher
> Upload your paper to Claude
>
> Ask: Create tool that converts citations between APA/MLA/Chicago
>
> Get interactive converter artifact
>
> Paste citation, get all three formats
>
> Never look up citation rules again

> [!note]- The Multi-Document Synthesizer
> Upload 10 papers to NotebookLM
>
> Ask: What do these papers disagree about? Where's the gap?
>
> Get literature review with identified conflicts
>
> Find your research question from what's missing
