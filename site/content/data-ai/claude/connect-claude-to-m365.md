## Overview

Claude can be connected to Microsoft 365 via the connector. This will allow your Claude instance to reference materials and gain context based on resources you may have in Microsoft 365.

The Claude Microsoft 365 connector will allow Claude to access Microsoft Teams messages, Outlook emails, Sharepoint and OneDrive documents, and more. (if you grant this access when configuring)

## Process

Follow the steps below to connect Microsoft 365 to Claude

1. Log into Claude under your Syracuse University account (*netid*@syr.edu)
2. Click your profile in the bottom left and select Settings

   ![A screenshot of the menu that appears when clicking the profile in the bottom left and a red box highlight the Settings option](./attachments/544210961/image-20251030-161518.png)
3. From here, press Connectors and next to the Microsoft 365 option press Connect

   ![Screenshot showing the Connectors option selected and the Microsoft 365 connector option](./attachments/544210961/image-20251030-161659.png)
4. Sign in, or select, your Syracuse University Microsoft account (*netid*@syr.edu)
5. You should be redirected back to Claude and have Microsoft 365 connected.
6. Feel free to press Configure next to the connector and change any options you desire or leave it configured as-is.
