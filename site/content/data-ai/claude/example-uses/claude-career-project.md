You can create and revise resumes, draft personalized cover letters, practice behavioral interview questions and research companies to prepare for internships or job applications.

Brief overview:

1. Select Career chat or create a Career project.
2. Upload your resume and job description.
3. Ask Claude: *Tailor my resume for this role* or *Write a cover letter based on my profile.*
