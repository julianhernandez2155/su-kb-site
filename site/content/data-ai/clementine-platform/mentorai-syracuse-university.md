# What is mentorAI

The mentorAI platform is a Syracuse University run AI platform that enables AI-powered personalized assistants for students, faculty, and staff. [Try Now!](https://mentor.ai.syr.edu/) (mentor.ai.syr.edu)

## 🎓 For Students: Personalized, Always-On Learning

**Create Your Own Study Mentor -**Build AI-powered tutors tailored to your learning goals, courses, and textbooks.

**Adaptive Learning Paths -**The AI adjusts to your pace and understanding, providing targeted feedback and curated practice questions.

**Verified Knowledge, Not Guesswork -**Every answer is grounded in your course materials, with transparent citations and relevant sources.

📈 **Benefits -**Higher engagement, deeper understanding, and better academic outcomes.

## 👩🏫 For Faculty: Control, Efficiency, and Empowerment

**Your AI Teaching Assistant -**Offload repetitive questions, grading prep, and FAQs to custom course-specific AI mentors.

**Review  Feedback -**Save time with AI-assisted assignment review and feedback, while maintaining full oversight.

**Curriculum Design Support -**Generate draft lesson plans, quizzes, and materials in minutes.

**Quality  Control -**Each mentor uses faculty-approved content only, ensuring integrity and accuracy.

🧠 **Benefits -** More time for teaching and research, Ability to focus on the things that enable deeper understanding with your student.
