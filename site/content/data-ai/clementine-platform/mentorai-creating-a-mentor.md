Creating a mentor profile on MentorAI is a straightforward process that involves entering essential information, selecting visibility options, and configuring interaction prompts. This allows mentors to effectively connect with students seeking guidance.

## Instructions

1. Click on the New Mentor button in your dashboard

![mentorAI home screen, highlighting the 'New Mentor' option on the left side of the screen. Under 'Mentors' and above 'New Project.'](./attachments/535068673/Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.44.43 PM.png)

1. **Input Mentor Information**

   1. **Name**

      - **Enter the Mentor's Name:** Fill in the name of the mentor you want to create.
      - **Purpose:** This will be the primary identifier for mentees looking to connect.
   2. **Description**

      - **Write a Brief Bio:** Provide a short description of the mentors background, skills, and what they can offer to mentees.
      - **Focus:** Include key experiences and the type of mentorship they provide (e.g., career advice, skill development).
   3. **Category**

      - **Select a Category:** Choose the appropriate category for the mentor based on their expertise (e.g., Astronomy, Art, Anthropology).
      - **Importance:** This helps mentees find mentors relevant to their interests.
   4. **Mentor Visibility**

      - **Choose Visibility Settings:** Decide whether the mentor will be visible to all students, select groups, or kept private.
      - **Consideration:** Think about how accessible you want the mentor to be for potential mentees.
   5. **Image**

      - **Upload a Profile Picture:** If applicable, upload a professional image of the mentor.
      - **Tip:** A picture can enhance engagement and make the profile more relatable.

!['Create Mentor' Settings screen showing options for 'Name,' 'Description,' 'Category,' and 'Image.'](./attachments/535068673/Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.45.00 PM.png)

1. **Configure Prompts**

   1. **System Prompts**

      - **Review Existing Prompts:** These are usually pre-populated with suggestions for how the mentor should interact with mentees.
      - **Editing:** Feel free to modify these prompts to better fit the mentors style or focus areas.
   2. **Proactive Prompts**

      - **Add Engagement Prompts:** These prompts can be used to initiate discussions with mentees.
      - **Customization:** Tailor these prompts to encourage specific types of interactions.
   3. **Guided Prompts**

      - **Set Guidance for Conversations:** These prompts guide the mentors responses in a structured manner.
      - **Tip:** Make them relevant to the mentors expertise and the mentees needs.

!['Create Mentor' Prompts screen showing options for 'System Prompts,' 'Proactive Prompts,' and 'Guided Prompts.'](./attachments/535068673/Screenshot 2025-11-11 at 1.45.17 PM.png)

1. Click the Save button to submit the new mentor profile.

> [!info]
> Add your configured prompts at the bottom of the default for more effective mentoring.
